I really like watching movies, to the point where going a full week without watching one feels almost unnatural. Most of my movie sessions happen at night, usually when everything is quiet and my brain is most willing to overthink plot details that probably were not meant to be overthought.
I am especially drawn to movies that keep me on the edge of my seat and refuse to let my mind rest. If a film makes me pause, rewind, or stare at the ceiling after it ends, it has already won. Thrillers and whodunit movies are my comfort zone, mainly because I enjoy guessing, doubting every character, and still being wrong by the end.
My favorite show is Dark, which says a lot about my tolerance for confusion and complex timelines. My favorite movie is Interstellar, a film I admire for making space, time, and emotions feel equally overwhelming.
